NEPO Tech and Engineering Scholarship for Nigerian Undergraduates 2026 | Application Guide

The NEPO Foundation is currently accepting applications for its 2026 Technical Excellence Grant. This initiative is designed for Nigerian students who are passionate about Engineering and Technology.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Institution: Must be a full-time undergraduate in a Nigerian University.
  • Field of Study: Open strictly to Engineering (All branches) and Technology (Computer Science, Software Engineering, IT, etc.) departments.
  • Academic Standing: Minimum 2.1 or equivalent CGPA.
  • Nationality: Open to Nigerian citizens only.

Benefits of the Award

The NEPO Scholarship provides comprehensive support to selected finalists:

  • ₦1,000,000 cash grant for 10 deserving students.
  • 5 High-performance Engineering Laptops for the top 5 candidates.
  • Access to exclusive tech workshops and job placements.
